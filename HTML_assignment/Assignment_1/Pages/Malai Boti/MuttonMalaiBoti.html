Mutton Malai Boti

        Mutton Malai Boti Recipe
Ingredients / Masalay

Mutton (boneless, choti botiyan) – ½ kg

Dahi – 3 tbsp

Fresh cream (malai) – 4 tbsp

Adrak-lehsan paste – 1 tbsp

Papaya paste – 1 tbsp (meat tender karne ke liye)

Lemon juice – 2 tbsp

Hari mirch – 2–3, bareek chopped

Hara dhaniya – 2 tbsp, chopped

Mozzarella ya cheddar cheese – 2 tbsp grated (optional, aur creamy taste ke liye)

White pepper powder – 1 tsp

Kali mirch powder – ½ tsp

Garam masala powder – ½ tsp

Namak – zaiqe ke mutabiq

Oil ya butter – 2 tbsp (basting ke liye)

Method / Tariqa

Mutton tender karna

Mutton ki botiyan ko ya to papaya paste ke sath marinate karen, ya phir halke paani mein 10–15 min boil kar ke soft kar lein.

Is se grill karte waqt mutton jaldi gal jata hai aur hard nahi hota.

Marination

Ek bowl mein dahi, malai, adrak-lehsan paste, papaya paste (agar boil nahi kiya),
 lemon juice, hari mirch, hara dhaniya, white pepper, kali mirch, garam masala aur namak dal kar mix karo.

Isme grated cheese bhi daal kar achhi tarah blend kar lo.

Mutton ki botiyan is masale mein dal kar overnight fridge mein rakho (kam az kam 6 ghante).

Skewers par lagana

Marinated mutton ko seekhon par lagao. Agar wooden skewers hain to unhe paani mein bhigo kar use karo.

Grill / BBQ / Oven

BBQ grill par ya oven (200°C) par 20–25 min tak cook karo jab tak mutton soft aur golden na ho jaye.

Beech beech mein butter ya oil brush karte raho.

Dhuaan dena ho to ek piece ko stove ki aag par rakh kar handi mein dhuaan kar lo.

Serve karna

Garam garam mutton malai boti ko hari chutney, paratha ya naan ke sath serve karo.
