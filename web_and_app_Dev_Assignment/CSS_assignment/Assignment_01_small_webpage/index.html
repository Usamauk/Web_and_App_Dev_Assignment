Khud Par Yakeen Rakho

Zindagi mein sabse bada sahara khud insaan khud hota hai. Aap jitni baar girte ho, utni hi baar uthane ki taqat bhi aapke andar hoti hai. Bas apne sapnon ko chhota mat samjho, aur apni mehnat se kabhi peecha mat hatao. Aaj nahi to kal, kamyabi zaroor milegi.
