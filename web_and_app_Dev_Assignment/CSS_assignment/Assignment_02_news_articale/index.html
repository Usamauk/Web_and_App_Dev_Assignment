PAKISTAN IT NEWS

Pakistan ka IT sector tezi se tarakki kar raha hai, jahan freelancing, software exports aur digital startups ka role barhta ja raha hai. Pakistan Software Export Board ke mutabiq IT exports mein har saal izafa ho raha hai, aur bohat se naye entrepreneurs web development, mobile apps, AI, aur cloud services jaisi fields mein international clients ke saath kaam kar rahe hain. Digital Pakistan initiative aur youth ke tech skills par focus ki wajah se naye training programs aur IT parks bhi ban rahe hain, jo naye opportunities aur jobs create kar rahe hain.

Dusri taraf, Pakistan mein IT companies ko kuch challenges ka samna bhi hai, jaise stable internet, tax policies, aur international payment gateways ki kami. Lekin government aur private organizations dono milkar in masail ko solve karne ki koshish kar rahe hain, jaise PayPal aur Stripe jaisi services ko Pakistan mein lane ke liye discussions ho rahe hain. Agar ye steps successfully implement ho jate hain, to Pakistan ka IT sector duniya ke top outsourcing hubs mein shamil ho sakta hai.
