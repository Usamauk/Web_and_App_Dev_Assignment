Method / Tariqa

Pyaz talna

Ghee ya tel garam karo, pyaz ko golden brown hone tak tal lo.

Adha nikal lo garnish ke liye, adha handi mein rehne do.

Mutton marinate karna

Ek bartan mein mutton, dahi, adrak-lehsan paste, lal mirch powder, dhaniya powder, haldi aur namak dal ke mix kar lo.

Isme adhi tali hui pyaz, hara dhaniya, pudina aur hari mirch bhi mila lo.

Kam az kam 2 ghante ke liye marinate karo (rat bhar rakho to aur behtareen taste).

Layering karna

Ek bhari handi (clay handi best hai) mein marinated mutton dal do.

Upar se bachi hui pyaz, dhaniya, pudina, whole spices, garam masala aur zafran wala doodh dal do.

Thoda ghee drizzle kar do.

Handi seal karna

Aata gundh kar lambi patli rope bana lo aur handi ke kinare lagakar dhakkan tight seal kar do taki steam bahar na nikle.

Dum par pakana

Bohat hi halki aanch par handi rakho (handi ke neeche tawa bhi rakh sakte ho jisse niche se na jale).

1.5 se 2 ghante dum par pakao jab tak mutton bilkul soft aur juicy na ho jaye.

Beech mein lid bilkul na kholo.

Serve karna

Table par le ja kar aata tod kar handi kholna (bohot royal presentation lagti hai).

Garam garam serve karo naan, roomali roti ya basmati chawal ke sath.
