Chicken Malai Boti

        Chicken Malai Boti Recipe
Ingredients / Masalay

Boneless chicken – ½ kg (choti boti pieces)

Dahi – 3 tbsp

Malai (fresh cream) – 4 tbsp

Adrak-lehsan paste – 1 tbsp

Hari mirch – 2, bareek chopped

Hara dhaniya – 2 tbsp, chopped

Lemon juice – 2 tbsp

White pepper powder – 1 tsp

Kali mirch powder – ½ tsp

Namak – zaiqe ke mutabiq

Mozzarella/cheddar cheese – 2 tbsp grated (optional lekin aur creamy taste ke liye best)

Oil ya butter – 2 tbsp (basting ke liye)

Method / Tariqa

Chicken marinate karna

Chicken ki boneless botiyan ek bowl mein dalo.

Usme dahi, malai, adrak-lehsan paste, hari mirch, hara dhaniya, lemon juice, white pepper, kali mirch, namak aur grated cheese dal kar mix karo.

Cling wrap karke fridge mein 2–3 ghante ke liye marinate hone do (rat bhar rakhne se aur behtareen taste).

Skewer par lagana

Chicken ki botiyan seekh par lagao (agar wooden skewers use kar rahe ho to 30 min paani mein bhigo lo taake jal na jaye).

Grill karna

BBQ grill par, tandoor mein ya oven (200°C) mein 15–20 minute tak grill karo.

Beech beech mein oil ya butter brush karte raho taake juicy aur soft rahe.

Gas stove par direct dhuaan bhi de sakte ho authentic BBQ taste ke liye.

Serve karna

Garam garam chicken malai boti ko hari chutney, paratha ya naan ke sath serve karo.
