Method:
1. Chashni (Sugar Syrup) Banana

Ek pan mein paani aur chini daal kar medium flame par boil karein.

Cardamom aur saffron daal dein.

Jab halki sticky consistency ho jaye (1-string nahi, bas thodi chipakne wali), tab band kar dein.

Rose water/kewra daal kar aside rakh dein (garam nahi, halka warm rakhiye).

2. Dough Banana

Milk powder, maida, suji aur baking powder ek bowl mein mix karein.

Ghee daal kar crumble jaisa bana lein.

Dheere dheere garam doodh daalkar soft aur smooth dough banaiye.

Dough ko zyada guthna nahi hai (warna jamun hard banenge).

10 min cover karke rest karne dein.

3. Balls Banana

Chhote equal size ke balls banayein (cracks na ho).

Agar cracks dikh rahe hain to haath par halka doodh laga kar smooth kar dein.

4. Frying

Oil/ghee ko medium-low heat par garam karein.

Balls daalkar dhimi aanch par fry karein (fast flame se bahar jal jayenge, andar kachhe rahenge).

Jab tak golden brown na ho jaye tab tak dhire dhire fry karte rahein.

5. Soaking

Fry kiye huye gulab jamun turant garam-garam chashni mein daal dein.
