Chicken Biryani

      Chicken Biryani Recipe (Serves 4–5)
Ingredients:
For the Chicken Marinade:

500g chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into medium pieces)

1 cup thick yogurt (curd)

2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)

1 tsp turmeric powder

2 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp garam masala powder

1 tbsp lemon juice

Salt to taste

For the Rice:

2 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 mins, drained)

4 cups water

2–3 cloves

2 green cardamoms

1 black cardamom (optional)

1 bay leaf

1-inch cinnamon stick

1 tsp salt

For the Biryani:

2 large onions (thinly sliced, fried golden brown—birista)

2 green chilies (slit)

2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee

2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

2 tbsp mint leaves (chopped)

A pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (or ½ tsp food color in milk)

Instructions:

Marinate Chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and salt.

Marinate at least 1 hour (overnight for best flavor).

Cook Rice

Boil water with whole spices and salt.

Add soaked rice, cook until 70% done.

Drain and set aside.

Cook Chicken

Heat oil/ghee in a large heavy-bottomed pan.

Add marinated chicken, cook until chicken is 80% done.

Layering the Biryani

In the same pot (or a handi), spread half the rice over the chicken.

Sprinkle half of fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk.

Add the remaining rice and repeat toppings.

Drizzle 1 tsp ghee on top.

Dum Cooking (Steaming)

Cover tightly (seal with dough if possible).

Cook on very low heat for 20–25 minutes.

Rest for 10 minutes before serving.
