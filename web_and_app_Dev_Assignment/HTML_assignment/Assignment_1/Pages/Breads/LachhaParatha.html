Instructions:
1. Dough Banana

20â€“30 minute rest karne de.

2. Paratha Roll Karna

Dough ka ek ball banayein aur belan se patla roti bel lein.

Ab upar se thoda ghee lagaiye aur halka sa atta chhidak dijiye.

Roti ko pleats ki tarah (jaise sari ko fold karte hain) fold karte jayein.

Ab isko roll karke gol ball banayein (jaise Swiss roll coil hota hai).

Thoda dabakar phir se bel lijiye (medium-thick).

3. Paratha Sekhna

Tawa garam karein.

Paratha ko dono side golden brown hone tak sekhiye, har baar thoda ghee lagate hue.

Jab crispy layers dikhne lagein, nikal lijiye.
